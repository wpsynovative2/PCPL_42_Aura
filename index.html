About the Project

A Home Designed for
Inspired Living

42 Aura (formerly Priyadarshini CHSL) is a premium residential redevelopment project situated on S.V. Road in Santacruz West, Mumbai a location that anchors you to the heart of the city while offering the serenity of a well-established neighbourhood.

Developed by PCPL a trusted name since 2013 this project combines contemporary architecture with thoughtfully curated amenities, delivering affordable luxury and everyday convenience to discerning families. The project offers excellent connectivity to the airport, Juhu Beach, BKC and the Western Express Highway.
